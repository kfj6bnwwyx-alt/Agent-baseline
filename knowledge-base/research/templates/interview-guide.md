# User Interview Guide Template

## Before the interview
- Define research questions (what you want to learn, NOT what you'll ask)
- Recruit 5-8 participants matching target persona
- Prepare recording consent form

## Warm-up (5 min)
- Introduce yourself and the purpose
- "There are no wrong answers — we're testing the product, not you"
- Ask about their role/background briefly

## Core exploration (30-40 min)
- Start broad, get specific
- "Tell me about the last time you..."
- "Walk me through how you currently..."
- "What's the hardest part about..."
- Follow interesting threads — deviate from script when needed

## Specific tasks (if applicable, 10-15 min)
- "Can you show me how you would..."
- Observe silently, note where they hesitate

## Wrap-up (5 min)
- "Is there anything I should have asked but didn't?"
- "Who else should I talk to?"
- Thank them

## After the interview
- Write summary within 24 hours while memory is fresh
- Tag key quotes, pain points, and surprises
- Add to synthesis board
