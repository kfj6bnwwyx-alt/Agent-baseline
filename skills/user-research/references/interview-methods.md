# Interview Methods

Adapted from designer-skills (MC Dean): interview-script, summarize-interview.

## Interview types

**Exploratory**: Understanding the problem space. Open-ended, follow threads.
**Evaluative**: Testing a specific solution. Task-based, observe behavior.
**Contextual**: In the user's environment. Watch them work, ask why.

## Key techniques
- **5 Whys**: Keep asking "why" to get past surface answers
- **Critical incident**: "Tell me about a time when..." for real examples
- **Think aloud**: User narrates their thought process during tasks
- **Silence**: Let pauses breathe — users fill them with honest answers
