# Persona Framework

Adapted from designer-skills (MC Dean): user-persona.

## Persona structure

```markdown
# [Persona name] — [archetype label]

## Demographics
- Age range, location, tech comfort level
- Job title/role, industry

## Goals
- Primary: [what they're ultimately trying to achieve]
- Secondary: [related goals]

## Frustrations
- [Pain point 1 — be specific, not generic]
- [Pain point 2]

## Behaviors
- How they currently solve this problem
- Tools they use, frequency of use
- Decision-making patterns

## Context of use
- Where: [environment, device, connectivity]
- When: [triggers, frequency, duration]
- With whom: [solo, collaborative, supervised]

## Quote (representative, not actual)
> "[Something this persona would say that captures their mindset]"
```

## Guidelines
- Base on research data, not assumptions
- 3-5 personas maximum per product
- Update quarterly or after major research
- Include anti-personas (who this product is NOT for)
